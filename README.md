### üöÄ Strategic Recommendations
#### Positive Business Opportunities

* Invest heavily in TV-PG & TV-MA categories for high satisfaction and retention.

* Expand high-quality TV series, especially multi-season formats.

* Promote hidden high-rated content to improve engagement at low cost.

* Clean metadata to enhance discoverability and parental controls.

* Increase localized content in underrepresented regions.

* Acquire content from historical rating peaks (1970s, 1980s, early 2000s).

* Diversify formats with micro-series and long-form documentaries.

#### Risks / Negative Growth Factors

* Overreliance on mediocre but popular titles may harm brand perception.

* Weak family-friendly library risks losing a valuable demographic.

* Metadata gaps reduce usability and parental control reliability.

* Production cycle "troughs" can cause future content shortages if not mitigated.
